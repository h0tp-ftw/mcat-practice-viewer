MCAT-Style Practice Viewer

An offline viewer for reviewing multiple-choice practice questions in a test-day-style interface. Everything runs in your browser.

About

This is an independent practice tool. It shows one passage-and-question set at a time in the familiar split-screen layout, with a highlighter, answer strike-out, a question navigator, a section timer, a periodic table, and worked explanations. Nothing is uploaded, installed, or tracked.

How it works

  • Use the Review Answer switch to choose a mode. With it off (Exam), you answer freely and see your results when you end the section. With it on (Tutor), confirming a question reveals the answer and explanation.
  • Highlight text with Alt+H, remove highlights with Alt+Shift+H, and click the × on a choice to cross it out. Flag a question with Alt+F, open the navigator with Alt+V, and the periodic table with Alt+T.
  • To use your own questions, drag a .json file onto the page or choose Load JSON. See the question-set format for how to build one.
